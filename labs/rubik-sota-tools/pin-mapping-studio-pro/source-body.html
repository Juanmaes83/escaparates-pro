about

A visual pin-mapping tool for image overlays across different crop ratios and layouts. Upload an image, place pins once, and instantly translate their positions and relative sizes between two views (for example desktop and mobile). Designed for hotspots, SVG markers, callouts, and any overlay system that needs accurate coordinates across changing aspect ratios and breakpoints.
